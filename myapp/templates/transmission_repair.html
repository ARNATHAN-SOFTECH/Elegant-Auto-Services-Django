The transmission is one of the most overlooked yet essential components of a vehicle. It is directly connected to the engine and transforms the engine’s combustion power into the momentum that drives the wheels, ensuring optimal performance and fuel efficiency.

Since the transmission plays a vital role in your car’s overall efficiency, make sure to schedule regular servicing and keep it in top condition with the expert technicians at Elegant Way Auto Maint.

If you notice grinding, ticking, or whining noises while your vehicle is in neutral, experience slipping gears, see your engine warning light illuminated, or feel shaking when changing gears, these could be warning signs of transmission problems—often caused by low or leaking transmission fluid. Ignoring these symptoms may lead to costly repairs, so early diagnosis is key. We recommend servicing your transmission approximately every 80,000 km and always using manufacturer-recommended, premium-quality transmission fluid to ensure peak performance
